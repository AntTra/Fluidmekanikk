##### Streamline, steady in-compressible flow:
>$\frac{P}{\rho g} + \frac{V^2}{2g} + z = const.$

##### Massebalanse: 
>$\frac{P_a}{\rho g} + \frac{V^2_a}{2g} + z_a = \frac{P_b}{\rho g} + \frac{V^2_b}{2g} + z_b + h_L$

##### Hydrostatisk trykk:
>$\frac{dP}{dz}=-\rho g$

Kan omformuleres til:
>$P_b - P_{atm} = \rho gh$

Total head loss:
>$h_{L,total} = h_{L, major} + h_{L, minor} = \sum f\frac{LV^2}{D2g}+\sum K_L \frac{V^2}{2g}$

# Variabler
---
## Termodynamikk

**P**  -  Pressure \[Pa\]  
**$\rho$**  -  Density \[$kg m^{-3}$\]
**T** -  Absolute temperature \[K\]
**R** -  Specific gas constant \[$J kg^{-1} K^{-1}$\] (e.g. air = 287)
**$\mu$**  -  Dynamic viscosity \[Pa s\]
**$\nu$**  -  $\frac{\mu}{\rho}$ \[$m² s^{-1}$\]

## Kinematikk (Velocity, acceleration)

**V**  -  Velocity vector \[$m s^{-1}$\] 
**u, v, w** -  Velocity components in x, y, z
**U**  -  Characteristic/mean velocity \[$m s^{-1}$\]  Dynamisk
**$\partial / \partial t$** -  local time derivative
**(V $* \nabla$)V** - Convective acceleration
**$\frac{D}{Dt}$** - Material derivative (Actual acceleration of a specific fluid particle)

**b** - width
